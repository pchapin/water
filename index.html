Waterfalls of the New England Region

My name is Peter Chapin, and this is my page for waterfall enthusiasts. I focus on the waterfalls of New England and neighboring regions (such as New York and bordering Canada). I pursue waterfalls as a hobby and as an excuse to get outside and into the woods. It's also an opportunity for me to practice with photography and website design. Enjoy the site; I hope you find it interesting and useful.
